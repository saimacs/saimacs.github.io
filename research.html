Current Research

I am affiliated with the USC Center for Energy Informatics, where I work on the Smart Grid project. My focus is on developing strategies for dynamic demand response (D2R) in the university campus microgrid. I am applying data-driven learning methods to model spatial and temporal energy consumption and curtailment patterns on campus. Such models have potential applications in energy use planning and conservation. For more information about my group's work, please visit our wiki page.

Previous Research

Previously, I was associated with the Cookie Group at the University of Ottawa. I did research in the area of sentiment analysis in informal text under the guidance of Prof. Stan Szpakowicz.
