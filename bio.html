Bio

I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at USC in Los Angeles, CA. I work under the guidance of Prof. Viktor Prasanna and Dr. Charalampos Chelmis. Previously, I was also mentored by Prof. Yogesh Simmhan.

At USC, my research is focused on developing data-driven models for dynamic decision making in smart grid applications. I am affiliated with the Smart Grid group, where I am working on the Los Angeles Smart Grid demonstration project.

Previously, I completed my Master's in Computer Science from the University of Ottawa, in Ottawa, Canada. I was associated with the Natural Language Processing (NLP) group, where I did research in the area of emotion analysis in text under the guidance of Prof. Stan Szpakowicz.

I grew up in Aligarh, India, and did my Bachelor's in Computer Engineering from the Aligarh Muslim University.
