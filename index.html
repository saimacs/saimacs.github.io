I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Southern California (USC) in May 2016. My advisor was Prof. Viktor Prasanna. During my Ph.D., I was also mentored by Dr. Yogesh Simmhan and Dr. Charalampos Chelmis. My Ph.D. research focused on developing AI methods and data-driven models for dynamic decision making in smart grid.
